5. INVESTIGATED "REGENERATE IS NOT WORKING"
-------------------------------------------
- Found the cause: the system only allows 3 content generations at once.
  Two old drafts (both for the Axxiom brand) had been STUCK in the
  "generating" state for about 9 hours (most likely a server restart caught
  them mid-generation). Those two zombies were holding the slots, so new
  regenerate/generate attempts get blocked with a "too many generating" error.
- Also found a deeper reason these get stuck: if anything fails AFTER the
  writing step (WordPress calls, image step, etc.), the draft can be left
  "generating" forever instead of being marked as needing review.
- The Claude model setting is fine (not the problem).
